### Interests

__

#### Numerical Modeling

Applying advanced numerical methods in various application areas in computational science and engineering, mainly in porous media mechanics

__

#### Programming

Developing codes for professional research and hobby projects mainly based on Python and C++

__

#### Academics

Teaching and supervision. Giving lectures, particularly on computational modeling courses, and supervising/co-supervising undergraduate and graduate students

__

#### Machine Learning and AI

Development of machine learning models and generative AI assistants for various practical problems in geomechanics, e.g. landslide risk assessment

__

#### Data Analysis and Visualization

Applying modern data analysis and visualisation tools and methods to engineering data for a better insight and content creation
