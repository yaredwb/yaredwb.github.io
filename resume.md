## Research Interests & Expertise

<div class="expertise-grid">
  <div class="expertise-item">

    <p>Development of machine learning models and generative AI assistants tailored to geotechnical challenges. Current applications focus on landslide risk assessment, mining optimization, and injection in tunneling, leveraging both physics-informed approaches and data-driven techniques.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="expertise-item">
    <h3>🔬 Computational Geomechanics</h3>
    <p>Applying advanced numerical methods (e.g., finite element methods, isogeometric analysis) to simulate fluid flow, heat flow and deformation in soils/rocks. Emphasis on practical engineering applications such as landslide risk assessment, climate adaptation of infrastructure foundations and geothermal energy.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="expertise-item">
    <h3>💻 Programming for Research & Applied Projects</h3>

  </div>
  
  <div class="expertise-item">
